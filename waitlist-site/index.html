About Go-Cycle

Go-Cycle is a pioneering green tech platform dedicated solely to the safe collection, management, and recycling of batteries across Nigeria. We believe that sustainable energy starts with responsible disposal.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between conscientious sellers and certified recycling facilities, ensuring zero toxic waste ends up in our landfills.
